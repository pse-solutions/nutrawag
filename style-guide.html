Futura
Used for body text and general content

Primary body text is 16px with 24px line height. This is used for most of the content on the website, providing good readability while maintaining a clean and professional appearance.

Secondary body text is 14px with 20px line height. This smaller text is used for things like product descriptions, disclaimers, and other supplementary information.

Icons

Icons should follow these guidelines:

  • Simple, outlined icons with 2px stroke weight
  • Corner radius of 2px for square icons
  • Consistent visual weight and style
  • Base size of 24px (16px for smaller elements)

Icon States & Sizes

XS - 16px
SM - 20px
MD - 24px
LG - 32px
XL - 48px
